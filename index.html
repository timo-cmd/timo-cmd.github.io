Hey, I'm Timo S. !

I am a young, dedicated computer science student who has a great passion for system-engineering and backend-solutions.

I got first confronted with technology when I was 14 years old. My first programming language was C/C++, with which I made a couple of apps... Since then I learned a lot of other languages and tools that I use at work. I am engaged at the construction of compilers and interpreters. To enhance my knowledge I studied a lot about concurrency and parallelism-models in languages and how they affect us at our work. My main interests are system-engineering, compiler-construction, dezentralized and distributed platforms and cyber-security. I am available on GitHub and I would be happy for some collaborations
